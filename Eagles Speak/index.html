Welcome To Emory Eagles Speak

Emory Eagles Speak (EES) was started by Ed Lee and a team of highly motivated undergraduate students at Emory University in October 2015. Ed Lee serves as the director for the club. Formerly Director of Debate and Department of Communications faculty at the University of Alabama, Ed Lee has coached debate at Emory University since 2004. Widely considered one of the most innovative argument coaches in the nation, Lee won the Ovid L. Davis award as coach of the 2007 NDT champions and the James J. Unger award for coaching the 2010 Copeland winners, the number one team in the country entering the National Debate Tournament.
